How to Resize Images

Stuart Zahn
pdf

Despite SaaS solutions like Cloudinary that can manipulate images for you in the cloud. You may still want to take advantage of free and open source libraries on your own servers. Using ImageMagick you can easily batch scale images.

mogrify -resize x250 -quality 80 -path ../thumbs *.jpg
